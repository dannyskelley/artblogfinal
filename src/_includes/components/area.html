🎉 Annual Events in Chesaning, MI

Chesaning Showboat Park Events

  • Chesaning Showboat Music Festival (Summer): A beloved tradition featuring live music, food vendors, crafts, and family activities.
  • River Days Festival: Celebrate summer with parades, fireworks, and community wide celebrations.
  • Car Shows & Tractor Pulls: Perfect for vintage vehicle fans and motorheads alike.
  • Halloween Spooktacular (October): Family friendly fall fun with costumes, treats, and hayrides.

Distance from hotel: 2 minutes drive

Saginaw County Fair – Chesaning

Held every August, this is one of Michigan’s best county fairs with livestock shows, carnival rides, food, and grandstand entertainment.

Distance: 5 minutes drive
